Michael Hettich was born in New York City and he grew up in that city and its suburbs. he has lived in upstate NY, Colorado, Northern Florida, Vermont and Miami, where he now lives. His most recent book of poetry, Like Happiness, has just been published by Anhinga Press. Other books include Swimmer Dreams (Turning Point, 2004) and Flock and Shadow: New and Selected Poems (New Rivers Press, 2005). A new book of poems, The Animals Beyond Us, is forthcoming from New Rivers in 2011. His poems have appeared in many journals, among them Orion, New Letters, Prairie Schooner, TriQuarterly, Smartish Pace and Poetry East.
